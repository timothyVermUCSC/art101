2000 - 2010

BEGINNING OF THE MODERN AGE

By the time the year 2000 came around, modern game consoles were ready to dominate the market. The Playstation 2, the Xbox, and the Gamecube were all released betweeen 2000-2001. These new consoles allowed for the new multiplayer era of games to gain their footing. Online gaming soon became default as massive multiplayer online games like world of warcraft became extremely popular. Consoles and PC's both began to include online play as a feature to many of their new titles.

THE RISE OF 3D GAMES

As the age of the Playstation and Xbox continued, we began to see some major developement in video game graphics and gameplay. In 2005 and 2006 the Playstation 3, the Xbox 360, and the Wii were all released. The Wii had less revolutionary graphics than the other two, but the motion controls were a massive hit. These new consoles also allowed new sequels and franchises start to come to light. Games like Uncharted 2, Grand Theft Auto 4, Call of Duty 4, and many other 3d games started to become very popular.

THE START OF MOBILE GAMES

Mobile games had been around for a couple years, but towards the end of the decade, around 2009, they began to gain traction as a very popular medium of gaming. The release of the Iphone in 2007 was a major catalyst of this. One of the most massive mobile games that was founded during this time was Angry Birds. It wasn't till the next decade where games like flappy bird and clash of clans were created and mobile games were at their most popular.
